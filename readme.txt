Embed videos in your website with our responsive HTML5 player. Upload new videos & manage existing ones from your wordpress admin.

== Description ==
With [api.video](https://api.video), managing videos on your WordPress website becomes a breeze. This powerful tool handles all the aspects of video management seamlessly. It automatically converts your videos into the optimal format, ensuring they look great on any screen. Not only that, but it also securely stores your videos and delivers them to viewers around the world using a reliable global network. To top it off, api.video offers a free, fully customizable web player, giving you complete control over how your videos are presented to your audience. Say goodbye to the complexities of video management and enjoy a hassle-free experience with api.video.

== Features ==
* Upload videos–up to 4k–directly from your Wordpress admin; they’ll be quickly encoded and ready for streaming in seconds.
* Select previously uploaded videos from your api.video library.
* Update video titles, descriptions, and even tags from your Wordpress admin.
* Embed videos with our responsive HTML5 player.
